CarJager, a French marketplace for classic and vintage cars, transitioned its site search from Algolia to Meilisearch Cloud, enabling its lean team to scale search capabilities across approximately 2,000 listings and 2,500 editorial articles without DevOps overhead. The migration resolved sorting limitations in their previous solution and helped sustain consistent search performance during high-traffic periods. The case study demonstrates how a managed search platform can simplify operations for product teams with limited engineering resources.
